Album • The Monster that lives on 2120 Samuel Drive

A concept album about what really happened inside 2120 Samuel Drive – narcissistic abuse, a mind breaking under it, and the fight to crawl back out.

The Monster that lives on 2120 Samuel Drive is my way of taking back the story. These songs, lyrics, and visuals come from my real experiences with mental illness, emotional and physical abuse, and the slow process of realizing what narcissistic behavior was doing to me. This album is the sound of me learning the truth, speaking it out loud, and choosing to keep going.

Contains discussion of mental health, emotional abuse, and suicidal thoughts.
